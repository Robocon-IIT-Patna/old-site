About Us

ABU Robocon

ABU Robocon, short for Asia-Pacific Broadcasting Union Robotic Contest, is an annual international robotics competition that brings together university students from different countries in the Asia-Pacific region. The competition aims to promote friendship and exchange among young people with similar interests in robotics and automation.

Each year, a new theme is announced for the competition, and participating teams are required to build robots that can complete specific tasks related to that theme. The teams are given a set of rules and limitations to follow, and they have to design, build, and program their robots accordingly to achieve the objectives of the game.
